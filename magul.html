Magul Maha viharaya

a

Situated in Lahugala national park the Monaragala district.It is situated in 14 km away from Potuvil town.

According to the Great Chronicle t this Temple was built by King Kavanthissa in the 2nd century BC on the place he married the pricess Vihara Maha devi.

After hearing a boat with a fair maiden was found ashore of Arugambay beach. A group of monks approaching boat and realize it was Princess Devi, daughter of King Kelanitissa of Maya Rata A, who sacrifice to stop brining the sea unto land. King Kavantissa heard went to meet her and later married her in keeping with tradition, and she became Queen Vihara Maha Devi. Later they built a temple and a monastery at the very same site.

The temple area is covered around 10,000 acres were large number of ruins are found. Among these ruins of magul maduwa -(place where the wedding ceremony took place) , palace,moonstone,monastery Stupa ponds are found. This is the only moonstone with the the carvings of elephants with their mahouts.and also the remains of a white marble Buddha statue .There is also a stone wall three to four feet in height can also be seen
