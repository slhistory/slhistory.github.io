Ruwanweli Seya

The Ruwanwelisaya is a stupa, situated in Anuradhapura Srilanka. It is sacred to Buddhists all over the world. This was built by King Dutugamunu in B.C. It is also known as Mahaseya,swaramali chithya and Rathnamali seya. Ruwanwelisaya is one of the 8 sacred places in the city of Anuradhapura. The original stupa has been about 180 feet in height It was a ruin in the 19th century and had been expanded and renovated by many kings .it was renovated in the early 20th century.The final crowning of the stupa took place on 17 June 1940. It is of one of th e world's tallest monuments, with the hight of 338 ft and the circumference of951 ft.

MagulMaha seya

Magul maha viharas an ancient Buddhist temple situated in Lahugala, was built by King Kawanthissa in the 2nd Century BC. This is the place where the king married the pricecess Vihara Maha Devi. The foundations of the “Magul maduwa” where the wedding ceremony took place in the vihara premises.Land of the vihara comlex is about 10,000 acres,ruins of a palace, moonstone , monastery, bo-maluwa, stupas, pond . are found scattered all over. These ruins are surrounded by the forests of the Lahugala National Park, which is an important habitat for animals such as elephants, sambars, deers, leopards and many endemic birds of Sri Lanka. This Vihara ra had been renovated by several kings There was about 2,000 monks inhabited the complex at some stage in history, which is evident to the largeness of the ancient temple. This is a histologically protected monument of the country.

Sithulpawwa

This is an ancient Buddhist monastery with history of more than 2200 years, Located in 18 km east from Katharagama, . Sithulpawwa is is also called Chiththala Pabbatha", which means the hill of the quiet mind. This ancient monastery with a history over 2200 years was a place of worship for devotees as well as a center of Buddhist education for Buddhist monks. This was built by King Kawanthissa.Thousands of Arhath bhikkus lived at this monastery. Large number of stupas.ruins of Buddha statues ,Bodhisathwstatues,,imege house and cave temples spred throughout land. Paintings of this monastery are belong to Anuradhapura era and the ruins of stone Buddha images, Awalokeswara Bodhisattva images, Image Houses, Circular Relic Houses are spread throughout the monastery premises. Apart from the main Sithulpawwa stupa there is another hill called the ‘Small Sithulpawwa . This contains similar stupa and buildings. Stupas have been built on peaks of this rocky mountain range .
